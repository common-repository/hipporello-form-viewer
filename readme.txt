Trello-powered WP Forms, Form builder, SMTP, Emails by Hipporello

== Description ==

Hipporello offers a comprehensive suite of forms tailored for a wide array of needs, from capturing contact requests and gathering specific information to collecting feedback, processing orders, and event registrations on your WordPress site. These forms integrate with Trello, making it easy to manage and track submissions. With the drag-and-drop form builder, you can create custom forms and embed them into your WP site. The easy-to-use interface simplifies the design process, allowing you to gather structured data.
In addition to collecting form submissions, Hipporello extends its functionality to ensure better communication between you and your form submitters. You can engage in email communication directly from Trello following a submission. This is made possible through Hipporello’s built-in SMTP support, ensuring easy and reliable delivery of your messages. For any questions, do not hesitate to contact our dedicated support team by starting live chat through our website.


= Seamless Trello integration =
Hipporello forms seamlessly integrate with Trello. Each form submission creates a new card in your Trello board allowing for easy management, storage and tracking of submissions.

= Emailing Form Submitters =
Hipporello’s built in email system allows you to send email messages to those who submit forms from inside Trello. This popular feature ensures no submission gets missed and all submissions and communication can be seen in the same platform easily.

Hipporello offers an all-in-one form solution for users of all skill levels and it ensures that creating and customizing forms is straightforward, even for complete beginners. The Hipporello team is ready to help with setup and the basics to speed up your onboarding.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to embed a form in Wordpress using Hipporello? =

- Install Hipporello’s Service Desk
- Go to Admin Panel > Forms. Edit a template or create a form from scratch.
- Go to form embed and copy embed code as instructed for WordPress.

= How to add contact form in Wordpress? =

- Install Hipporello’s Service Desk
- Go to Admin Panel > Forms. Edit the Contact form template and save it.
- Go to form embed and copy embed code as instructed for WordPress.

= How to add captcha to Wordpress contact form? =
- Install Hipporello’s Service Desk
- Go to Admin Panel > Forms. Click on Contact form to edit it.
- Choose captcha from the form elements list on the right hand side. Save.
- Go to form embed and copy embed code as instructed for WordPress.

= Can I send emails with Hipporello forms? =
Yes, you can send as many emails as you wish. You can also define automated messages to send confirmation emails to those that contact you through forms. It’s possible to decide what email address you’d like your emails to be sent from. This way, you get a shared inbox to manage form submissions and you can collaborate with your team members on that.
